How to Play Ludo Master:

Roll the Dice: Tap the dice to roll and move your tokens based on the number you get.

Strategize: Move your tokens wisely to block opponents and advance your own. Utilize strategic moves to gain an advantage over your opponents.

Capture Tokens: Land on an opponent's token to send it back to the start. Capturing tokens is a key strategy to ensure your path to victory.

Reach Home: Get all your tokens to the center of the board to win the game. The first player to achieve this goal is declared the Ludo Master.
